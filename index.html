Simple Workout Routine

A daily set of exercises to stay active and healthy.

Warm-Up

  • Arm Circles 30 sec
  • Jumping Jacks 1 min
  • Neck Rolls 30 sec

Strength

  • Push-ups 10 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats 15 reps
  • Plank 30 sec

Cardio

  • High Knees 1 min
  • Butt Kicks 1 min
  • Burpees 10 reps

Cool Down

  • Toe Touch Stretch 30 sec
  • Quad Stretch 30 sec each leg
  • Deep Breathing 1 min
